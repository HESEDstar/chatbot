About Hesed
HESED Edusuite is a cloud-based school management and administration platform designed to digitize, automate, and centralize academic and administrative operations for primary and secondary institutions. The platform helps schools improve efficiency, transparency, communication, and data-driven decision-making.
HESED Edusuite operates on a subscription-based pricing model, charged per student per term, with tiered plans to match different levels of institutional needs and digital maturity.

Core Purpose
The platform exists to:
* Reduce manual and paper-based school operations
* Improve academic record accuracy and accessibility
* Strengthen communication between school administrators, teachers, students, and parents
* Provide actionable academic and financial insights
* Support scalable digital transformation for schools

User Roles & Access Control
HESED Edusuite uses role-based access control, ensuring users only see features relevant to their role. Typical roles include:
* School Administrators
* Teachers
* Finance Officers / Accountants
* Students
* Parents / Guardians
Each role has customized permissions and dashboards.
